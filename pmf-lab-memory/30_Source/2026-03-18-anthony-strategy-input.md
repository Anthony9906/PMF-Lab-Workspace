# Anthony Strategy Input

## Date

- 2026-03-18

## Source type

- strategy input
- operating constraints
- validation mechanism

## Positioning

- the team is an early validation engine for new business opportunities
- the near-term goal is to prove that the team can find opportunities, run validation, and produce investment recommendations
- the goal is not to start with a large product build

## Team responsibility

- continuously research AI + manufacturing opportunities
- output decision-grade validation conclusions
- turn high-potential opportunities into incubatable new business options
- accumulate opportunity maps, validation methods, ROI models, and handoff assets

## Current-stage strategy

- team size is two people, with limited budget and sample access bandwidth
- prioritize small, near-money, easy-to-validate scenarios
- run one primary project toward closed-loop validation
- keep A-class learning validation and C-class high-quality falsification in parallel
- build the mechanism and cadence first, then tighten the value anchor

## Innovation mechanism

- opportunity pool
- scoring card and first-pass screening
- formal validation through research, prototype, pilot, and ROI measurement
- A / B / C outcome framework
- continuation, incubation, pause, or stop-loss decision

## Opportunity pool management

- target 10 opportunity points
- first-pass screen 6 opportunities into a value-difficulty matrix
- produce a 3-month validation roadmap and a 6-month reserve roadmap
- current named primary-project example: standard-part selection recommendation to procurement cost optimization

## Opportunity scoring weights

- strategic fit: 25%
- closeness to money: 25%
- validation difficulty: 20%
- replicability: 15%
- asset accumulation value: 15%

## Outcome definitions

### A-class

- cognitive validation result
- key assumptions validated
- complete evidence package formed
- no independent commercialization loop yet

### B-class

- product closed-loop validation result
- real-scene validation completed
- prototype, feedback, and ROI estimate available
- meets incubation application threshold

### C-class

- high-quality falsification result
- logic and data show current investment is not worthwhile
- restart conditions are clarified
- helps avoid wrong investment

## Three-month OKR

### O1 mechanism

- build the AI + manufacturing opportunity pool
- capture 10 opportunities
- first-pass screen 6 opportunities
- form a 3-month and 6-month roadmap

### O2 primary project

- complete one first-round pilot for an AI + manufacturing scenario
- build MVP or prototype and enter a real business scene
- conduct at least 8 key user or business interviews
- decide whether to continue toward B, or classify as A or C

### O3 knowledge assets

- achieve at least one A-class result
- achieve at least one C-class result
- build an opportunity repository and knowledge asset repository

## Expected three-month deliverables

- a working innovation mechanism
- one clear AI + manufacturing validation main line
- one first-round pilot conclusion for the main project
- at least two knowledge assets: one A and one C
- one 3-month roadmap and one 6-month reserve roadmap
- evidence package, ROI framework, and handoff-ready project materials

## Management asks

- unrestricted token budget
- acceptance of the innovation evaluation rubric, not only short-term revenue
- support for enterprise data samples, research, and pilot resources
- fast review and incubation decision path for B-class projects
- explicit alignment on OKR points and incubation reward
