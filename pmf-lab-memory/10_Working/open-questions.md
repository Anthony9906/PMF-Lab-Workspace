# Open Questions

Questions that block prioritization, design, or validation.

## Typical blockers

- Who is the real buyer?
- Which workflow is the real pain point?
- What data exists today?
- What is the acceptable human review burden?
- What is the minimum PMF signal?

## Current kickoff questions

- Which manufacturing segments should we prioritize first: discrete assembly, machining, quality, supply chain, process manufacturing, or factory engineering?
- Are we targeting software-only wedges first, or are we open to hardware-adjacent workflows if the workflow pain is strong?
- What level of deployment friction is acceptable in the first 90 days: browser-based copilots, on-prem integration, edge inference, or ERP/MES plug-ins?
- What counts as an early PMF signal for this lab: repeated interviews, data-sharing, pilot interest, paid design partner interest, or actual budget?
- What is the preferred wedge buyer to start with: plant manager, quality leader, process engineer, production planner, sales engineer, or founder/GM of an SME manufacturer?
